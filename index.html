The skin concerns we see most often

We work every day with people who are tired of dealing with long-standing skin issues and just want a clear, realistic plan.

Acne & scarring

Adult and teen acne, persistent breakouts, clogged pores and post-inflammatory marks.

  • Combination of topical and oral treatments
  • Customized chemical peels
  • Goal: calm, predictable skin all month long

Redness & sensitivity

Rosacea, visible vessels, easily irritated skin or reactions to products and masks.

  • Detailed evaluation of vascular changes
  • Home care routine tailored to your skin
  • Guidance for gentle everyday skincare

Mole checks & prevention

Full-body skin exams, mole mapping and risk assessment based on your history.

  • High-resolution dermoscopy
  • Clinical photos to monitor changes over time
  • Sun-safety advice that fits your lifestyle

Dermatitis & allergies

Eczema, atopic skin, contact allergies, unexplained rashes or flares.

  • Investigation of potential triggers
  • Step-by-step treatment and skin-care plan
  • Recommendations for safe products

Aesthetic dermatology without the drama

We treat lines and laxity like any other medical concern: with moderation, a plan and the goal of a face that simply looks rested.

Botox for expression, not “frozen” faces Filler only where it truly helps
  • Short assessment, not a sales pitch. Before any aesthetic treatment, we explain what actually suits your features.
  • Small adjustments, natural results. The goal is to hear “you look well,” not “what did you do?”.
  • Combined with medical care. We address sensitivity, acne and pigmentation at the same time.

Informed, gradual changes – no surprises, no overdone results.

What it’s like to start with us

2

Board-Certified Dermatologist

Dr. Papadopoulos is a board-certified dermatologist with training in Europe and the U.S. and extensive experience in private practice. Her philosophy: every skin is a long-term record – it needs observation, time and consistency.

  • Special interest in adult acne and complex cases
  • Experience with sensitive and rosacea-prone skin

Her goal is calm, functional skin you can live in comfortably – not a one-off “perfect” photo.

Do I need a referral?

No. You can book directly, whether you’re using insurance or self-pay.

Can treatment start the same day?

In many cases yes, as long as it’s medically appropriate and fits with any current medications.

Do you offer virtual visits?

For follow-ups and select issues, tele-dermatology may be an option when it’s safe and clinically appropriate.
